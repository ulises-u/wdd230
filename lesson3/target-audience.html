Target Audienc

My target audience is people that want to know what businesses are present in their area. Nauvoo IL is a small city that's overflowing with history and culture. Those that want access to valuable resources, discounts, and relationships that help people and businesses save money and market their products.

Personas & Scenarios

person 1

Maria 35-year-old woman who has 3 children. She needs to find activities for different ages of her children before she has been in place and needs to shop online, so She confidently uses her iPad, and Android phone to access the web - whatever is to hand.

person2

Pedro is 32-year-old programmer who is Connected to the Internet all day, but he wants to visit and learn more about the history of Navoo, but he doesn't have a family, he travels alone, so he is interested in seeing all possible shops and markets around his place

Scenarios

"Ezequiel is a businessman who is tired after driving his car for several hours after a long day. He wants to go to the best restaurant in his neighborhood, especially since he has little time for breakfast, lunch or dinner."

"Rosita is a 55-year-old woman who is receiving her grandchildren tonight, she needs to get to the nearest market as soon as possible to buy groceries"

"Chris is a young man. He's new to the neighborhood and would love to see all the businesses in your community and their prices and especially the entertainment."

"Joanna is a young woman who is concerned about her weight. She wants to see if there is healthy food that she can buy for her at her house or if there are physical activities so she can train."
