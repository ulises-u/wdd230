Welcome to Nauvoo-a city rich with history and culture. Once the home of Sauk and Fox Indians, the early 1800's saw the beginning of a steady stream of European immigrants. Some stayed a few years, some stayed for generations, but each brought to this area their dreams, culture, and beliefs. Their legacy offers a rich experience that brings visitors from all over the world.
